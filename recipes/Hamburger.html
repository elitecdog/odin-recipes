Hamburger Recipe

How To Make A Simple Hamburger

This recipe will revolve around having ground beef, so if you don't have any go out to the store and get some first!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Burger Buns/Bread
  • Cheese
  • Condoments

Steps

  1. Scoop out a 1/4 pound ball of ground beef
  2. Flatten the patty until it's about half an inch thick
  3. Turn your burner on to a medium temperature and wait for the pan to heat up.
  4. Grill your burger on the pan until it reaches a dark brown color
  5. Take the burger off the grill/pan, put it on your bun, add condoments, and enjoy!
