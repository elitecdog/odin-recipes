Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

How To Make The Most Delicious Pepperoni Pizza

Making a good pizza is a staple of any kitchen/household. The first thing you're going to want to do is pre heat the oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Sauce
  • Pizza Dough
  • Pepperonis
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Role out your pizza dough until it's your desired thickness
  2. Lightly coat your dough with sauce until it's evenly distributed
  3. Layer on a hefty amount of cheese
  4. Distribute your pepperoni's across the top
  5. Bake for 20 minutes
