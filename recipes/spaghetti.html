Spaghetti Recipe

How To Make Spaghetti

This recipe will revolve around boiling water, so make sure to set it to a boil while you prep your ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesean

Steps

  1. Place your noodles into the boiling water. Let them cook for 10-12 minutes.
  2. Drain the water from your noodles and let them cool a bit.
  3. Apply your spaghetti sauce and stir it in.
  4. Scoop an appropriate amount onto a plate.
  5. Sprinkle some parmesean on top and enjoy!
